## Part 1: 根据中文提示写英文

1. 我叫贝丝·柯尔，但这不是我的故事。  
   → My name is Bess Curl, but ________________________.

2. 她写下了这个故事，然后把它交给了我。  
   → She wrote the story, and then ________________________.

3. “求您了，陛下，离开那扇窗户吧。”  
   → “Please, ________________________, come away from that window.”

4. 因为伊丽莎白女王害怕你。  
   → Because Queen Elizabeth ________________________ you.

5. 她恨我，因为我美丽，而她不是。  
   → She hates me because ________________________, and she is not.

6. 我上次见他时，他才十个月大。  
   → He was only ten months old ________________________.

7. 你为什么不写下来告诉他呢？  
   → ________________________ write and tell him?

8. 在我死之前，我想给我的儿子詹姆斯写信。  
   → But before I die, I ________________________ to my son, James.

<details>
<summary>点击查看 Part 1 答案</summary>

1. My name is Bess Curl, but **this is not my story**.
2. She wrote the story, and then **she gave it to me**.
3. “Please, **your majesty**, come away from that window.”
4. Because Queen Elizabeth **is afraid of** you.
5. She hates me because **I am beautiful**, and she is not.
6. He was only ten months old **when I last saw him**.
7. **Why don't you** write and tell him?
8. But before I die, I **would like to write** to my son, James.

</details>

## Part 3: 完成句子 (根据上下文填入恰当内容)

1. “Queens don't kill queens.”  
   “Don't they, Bess? Then why are we here, ________________________?”

2. “And Elizabeth has no children, so when she is dead, ________________________...”

3. “He needs to know the true story. ________________________?”

4. “Yes, I am, Bess. You know what is going to happen. One day soon, a man is going to bring a letter from Queen Elizabeth, and then ________________________.”

5. “But perhaps people tell him things ________________________. You know what people say.”

6. Mary's face went white. She was very angry, and for a minute ________________________.

<details>
<summary>点击查看 Part 3 答案</summary>

1. “Don't they, Bess? Then why are we here, **in this prison**?”
2. “And Elizabeth has no children, so when she is dead, **my son James...**”
3. “He needs to know the true story. **Why don't you write and tell him?**”
4. “One day soon, a man is going to bring a letter from Queen Elizabeth, and then **her men are going to kill me**.”
5. “But perhaps people tell him things **that are untrue**.”
6. Mary's face went white. She was very angry, and for a minute **I was afraid**.

</details>

## Part 4: 用正确的时态或形式填空

1. She wrote the story, and then she ________ (give) it to me.  
2. I ________ (go) to give it to her son.  
3. “Please, your majesty, ________ (come) away from that window,” I said.  
4. “Queens don't kill queens.” “Don't they, Bess? Then why ________ we ________ (be) here, in this prison?”  
5. “Because Queen Elizabeth is afraid of you.” “That's right,” Mary said. “She ________ (be) afraid of me, and she ________ (hate) me too.”  
6. He ________ (be) only ten months old when I last saw him.  
7. “He needs ________ (know) the true story.”  
8. “I'm going to write to James and tell him the true story. You can give it to him when I ________ (be) dead.”  
9. “You aren't going ________ (die).”  
10. “But before I die, I would like ________ (write) to my son, James.”

<details>
<summary>点击查看 Part 4 答案</summary>

1. She wrote the story, and then she **gave** it to me.
2. I **am going** to give it to her son.
3. “Please, your majesty, **come** away from that window,” I said.
4. “Queens don't kill queens.” “Don't they, Bess? Then why **are** we **here**?”
5. “Because Queen Elizabeth is afraid of you.” “That's right,” Mary said. “She **is** afraid of me, and she **hates** me too.”
6. He **was** only ten months old when I last saw him.
7. “He needs **to know** the true story.”
8. “I'm going to write to James and tell him the true story. You can give it to him when I **am** dead.”
9. “You aren't going **to die**.”
10. “But before I die, I would like **to write** to my son, James.”
