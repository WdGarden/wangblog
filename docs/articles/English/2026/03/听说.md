# 【115天练习计划】英语口语听力提升（Level 1）

## Day 20: Working Outside (户外劳作)

| 英文原文 | 中文翻译与解析 |
| :--- | :--- |
| Working outside today, I was working outside. | 今天在户外干活，我一直在户外忙碌。 |
| It was a sunny day and I was very hot. | 那是个晴天，我觉得很热。 |
| I wore a hat on my head, I watered all the plants, I weeded the flower beds, I cut back all of the plants that were growing too big. | 我头上戴了顶帽子，给所有植物浇了水，给花坛除了草，把长得太茂盛的植物都修剪了一番。<br>🔤 **词汇与短语**：<br>- **water** /ˈwɔːtə/ v. 给……浇水。<br>- **weed** /wiːd/ v. 除草，拔草。<br>- **cut back**：修剪，砍掉（过长的枝叶）。 |
| I gave some of the plants plant food, I cut the lawn, I raked the lawn. | 我给一些植物施了肥，修剪了草坪，还把草坪上的落叶耙干净了。<br>📝 **注意**：<br>- **plant food**：植物肥料。<br>- **cut the lawn** = **mow the lawn**：修剪草坪。<br>- **rake** /reɪk/ v. 用耙子耙，耙拢。 |
| I filled up the bird baths with water, I swept the sidewalks and the path, I took out the garbage. | 我给鸟浴盆加满了水，扫了人行道和小路，倒了垃圾。<br>🔤 **词汇**：<br>- **bird bath**：花园里供鸟儿饮水沐浴的浅盆。<br>- **sidewalk** /ˈsaɪdwɔːk/ n. (美)人行道。<br>- **take out the garbage**：倒垃圾。 |
| I filled the car up with gas, I washed the car, I hung out the clothes on the clothesline, I washed down the lawn furniture, I washed all of the windows on the house from the outside. | 我给车加满了油，洗了车，把衣服晾在晾衣绳上，冲洗了庭院家具，还把房子外面所有的窗户都擦了。<br>🔤 **词汇与短语**：<br>- **fill up with gas**：加满汽油。<br>- **hang out**：晾晒（衣物）。<br>- **clothesline** /ˈkləʊðzlaɪn/ n. 晾衣绳。<br>- **wash down**：冲洗，彻底清洗。<br>- **lawn furniture**：庭院家具（户外桌椅等）。 |
| I was so tired. So I had a glass of lemonade. | 我累极了。于是喝了一杯柠檬水。 |
| I talked to my neighbor and I helped her trim a tree. | 我和邻居聊了会儿天，还帮她修剪了一棵树。<br>🔤 **词汇**：<br>- **trim** /trɪm/ v. 修剪，修整。 |
| I planted some bulbs, and then I went into the house. I was exhausted. | 我种了些球茎植物，然后进了屋。我筋疲力尽了。<br>🔤 **词汇**：<br>- **bulb** /bʌlb/ n. 球茎（如郁金香、水仙花的种球）。<br>- **exhausted** /ɪɡˈzɔːstɪd/ adj. 筋疲力尽的。比 *tired* 程度更深。 |

## Day 21: Daily Schedule (每日日程)

| 英文原文 | 中文翻译与解析 |
| :--- | :--- |
| I wake up every morning at 7 o'clock. I take a shower, I eat my breakfast, I usually have toast or cereal, I brush my teeth, I put on my clothes, I catch the school bus, I ride to school. | 我每天早上7点起床。我冲个澡，吃早餐，通常吃吐司或麦片，刷牙，穿上衣服，赶上校车，坐车去学校。<br>🔤 **词汇与短语**：<br>- **take a shower**：淋浴。<br>- **cereal** /ˈsɪəriəl/ n. 麦片，谷物早餐。<br>- **catch the bus**：赶公交车（强调赶上）。<br>- **ride to school**：乘车去学校。 |
| In my class, we have math and English before recess. | 在班上，课间休息前我们要上数学和英语课。<br>📝 **词汇**：<br>- **recess** /rɪˈses/ n. (学校的)课间休息。 |
| At recess time, the girls skip or walk around and talk. The boys play in the playground or play baseball. | 课间休息时，女生们跳绳，或者四处走走聊聊天。男生们在操场上玩耍或打棒球。<br>🔤 **词汇**：<br>- **skip** /skɪp/ v. 跳绳。<br>- **walk around**：四处走动。<br>- **playground** /ˈpleɪɡraʊnd/ n. 操场，游乐场。 |
| After recess, we have physical education and geography. | 课间休息后，我们上体育课和地理课。<br>📝 **缩写**：<br>- **physical education**：体育课，常缩写为 **P.E.** |
| We eat lunch, and then we play outside. When the bell rings, we line up to go back into the classroom. | 我们吃午餐，然后在户外玩耍。铃响时，我们排队回教室。<br>🔤 **短语**：<br>- **line up**：排队，排成一列。<br>- **go back into**：回到……里面。 |
| After lunch, we have history and science. | 午餐后，我们上历史课和科学课。 |
| At recess, we play ball again, some of the girls play ball too. | 又一个课间休息时，我们又打球了，有些女生也打球。<br>📝 **注意**：<br>- 此处 *recess* 指下午的课间休息。 |
| In the winter we build snowmen. If it is too cold, we stay indoors and talk to each other. | 冬天我们堆雪人。如果天气太冷，我们就待在室内，互相聊天。<br>🔤 **词汇**：<br>- **build snowmen**：堆雪人。<br>- **stay indoors**：待在室内。<br>- **talk to each other**：互相交谈。 |
| After recess we have music and health. | 课间休息后，我们上音乐课和健康课。 |
| We get out of school at 3:30, I sometimes walk home with my friends or I take the bus, I have a snack and change my clothes when I get home. | 我们3点半放学，我有时和朋友步行回家，或者坐公交车。到家后，我吃点零食，换身衣服。<br>🔤 **短语**：<br>- **get out of school**：放学。<br>- **have a snack**：吃点心，吃零食。 |
| If it is raining, I watch television. If it is nice outside, I play with my friends. | 如果下雨，我就看电视。如果外面天气好，我就和朋友一起玩。<br>📝 **条件状语从句**：<br>- **If it is raining... If it is nice...** 用 *if* 引导，表示“如果……就……”。 |
| I have supper at 5:30. On some nights, I help my mother to do the dishes. | 我5点半吃晚餐。有些晚上，我帮妈妈洗碗。<br>🔤 **词汇**：<br>- **supper** /ˈsʌpə/ n. 晚餐（与 dinner 同义，多用于家庭场合）。<br>- **do the dishes**：洗碗。 |
| After supper I do my homework, I wash my face and hands and brush my teeth, I change into my pajamas, I go to bed at 9 o'clock. | 晚饭后我做作业，洗脸、洗手、刷牙，换上睡衣，9点上床睡觉。<br>🔤 **短语**：<br>- **do my homework**：做作业。<br>- **change into**：换上（衣服）。<br>- **pajamas** /pəˈdʒɑːməz/ n. 睡衣裤。<br>- **go to bed**：上床睡觉。 |
