# 中国愿协助推动阿富汗与巴基斯坦和解 - 语言点分析

| 英文原文 | 中文翻译与语言点解析 |
| :--- | :--- |
| China ready to help with Afghan-Pakistani reconciliation | 中方表示愿协助推动阿巴和解<br>🔤 **标题词汇**：<br>**reconciliation** /ˌrekənsɪliˈeɪʃn/ n. **和解，调和**。指使双方恢复友好关系，是外交和政治中的高频词。 |
| By ZHAO JIA \| chinadaily.com.cn \| Updated: 2026-03-16 16:41 | 作者：赵佳 \| 来源：中国日报网 \| 更新时间：2026-03-16 16:41<br>📝 **新闻电头**：此为新闻**署名行**（byline）与**时间戳**，标明作者、来源及最后更新时间。 |
| China said on Monday that it is ready to continue contributing to reconciliation and the easing of tensions between Afghanistan and Pakistan. | 中国3月16日表示，愿继续为阿富汗和巴基斯坦之间的和解与局势缓和作出贡献。<br>🔤 **核心表达**：<br>**is ready to**：**愿意，准备好**。正式声明中常用，表达积极姿态。<br>**contributing to**：**为…作出贡献**。`contribute to sth` 是高频搭配。<br>**the easing of tensions**：**紧张局势的缓和**。`ease` 作动词意为“减轻，缓和”，此处名词化。 |
| Foreign Ministry spokesman Lin Jian made the remarks at a regular press briefing, saying that China has been working through its own channels to mediate in the ongoing conflict between the two countries. | 外交部发言人林剑在例行记者会上作出上述表态。他指出，中方一直通过自身渠道为推动两国当前冲突的调解进行努力。<br>🔤 **重点词汇**：<br>**regular press briefing**：**例行记者会**。`briefing` 指简报会、发布会。<br>**through its own channels**：**通过自身渠道**。`channel` 这里指外交或沟通渠道。<br>**mediate** /ˈmiːdieɪt/ vi./vt. **调解，斡旋**。后常接 `in` 或 `between`。<br>**ongoing conflict**：**当前的冲突，持续中的冲突**。`ongoing` 强调正在进行。 |
| In recent days, China's Foreign Minister Wang Yi has held separate phone calls with Pakistani Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Mohammad Ishaq Dar and Afghan Foreign Minister Mawlawi Amir Khan Muttaqi, while China's special envoy for Afghan affairs has conducted shuttle diplomacy between the two countries. | 近日，中国外交部长王毅分别同巴基斯坦副总理兼外交部长穆罕默德·伊沙克·达尔、阿富汗外交部长阿米尔·汗·穆塔基通电话。与此同时，中国政府阿富汗事务特使也在两国之间开展了穿梭外交。<br>🔤 **动词与术语**：<br>**held separate phone calls**：**进行了分别通话**。`separate` 强调与各方单独沟通。<br>**special envoy**：**特使**。指为特定外交任务任命的代表。<br>**conducted shuttle diplomacy**：**开展穿梭外交**。指特使在冲突方之间往复奔走调解，是形象的外交术语。 |
| The Chinese embassies in both Afghanistan and Pakistan have also kept lines of communication open, Lin said. | 林剑表示，中国驻阿富汗和巴基斯坦使馆也保持着畅通的沟通渠道。<br>🔤 **习惯表达**：<br>**kept lines of communication open**：**保持沟通渠道畅通**。这是外交场合关于维持对话的固定表述。 |
| Both Afghanistan and Pakistan have expressed appreciation for China's active mediation efforts, he added. | 他补充说，阿富汗和巴基斯坦双方都对中方积极的调解努力表示赞赏。<br>🔤 **动词短语**：<br>**expressed appreciation for**：**对…表示赞赏**。`appreciation` 比 `thanks` 更正式。<br>**active mediation efforts**：**积极的调解努力**。`efforts` 常用复数表示多方努力。 |
| Lin noted that Afghanistan and Pakistan are neighbors that cannot move away from each other, and any problems between them can only be resolved through dialogue and consultation. | 林剑强调，阿富汗和巴基斯坦是搬不走的邻居，双方之间的任何问题只能通过对话协商解决。<br>🔤 **比喻与原则**：<br>**cannot move away**：**无法搬离**。用比喻强调地缘上的永久相邻性。<br>**resolved through dialogue and consultation**：**通过对话与协商解决**。阐述中方主张的外交解决路径。 |
| The immediate priority is to prevent the fighting from expanding and to bring the two sides back to the negotiating table as soon as possible, he said. | 他表示，当务之急是防止冲突扩大，推动双方尽快回到谈判桌前。<br>🔤 **外交术语**：<br>**immediate priority**：**当务之急，最优先事项**。<br>**bring ... back to the negotiating table**：**将…带回谈判桌**。经典外交用语，意指重启正式谈判。 |
| China hopes the two sides will remain calm and restrained, engage in face-to-face communication at an early date, achieve a ceasefire as soon as possible and resolve their differences through dialogue, Lin added. | 林剑进一步指出，中方希望双方保持冷静克制，早日开展面对面沟通，尽快实现停火，通过对话解决分歧。<br>🔤 **系列动词**：<br>**remain calm and restrained**：**保持冷静克制**。`restrained` 指克制、不冲动。<br>**engage in face-to-face communication**：**进行面对面沟通**。<br>**achieve a ceasefire**：**实现停火**。`ceasefire` 指（通常暂时的）停火协议。<br>**resolve differences**：**解决分歧**。`difference` 常用复数指意见或利益分歧。 |
| In recent weeks, scores of people from both sides have been killed or injured in the conflict between Afghanistan and neighboring Pakistan, according to officials from both countries. | 根据两国官方消息，近几周来，阿富汗与邻国巴基斯坦之间的冲突已造成双方数十人伤亡。<br>🔤 **数量与背景**：<br>**scores of**：**数十个，许多**。`score` 指20，`scores of` 表示几十个，数量多于 `dozens of`。<br>**according to officials**：**根据官员/官方说法**。点明伤亡数据的信源。 |
